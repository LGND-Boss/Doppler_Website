Why we're here

Doppler is small, deliberate, and built on craft. We hire slowly — and once you're in, we invest in you. Beans, training, equipment, time to study, time to think.

The promise.

Mastery, not headcount.

Six people on the floor, not sixteen. We pay for skill and we expect it. You'll cup every Monday and we'll send you to a coffee origin within your first 24 months.

Slow rules.

No dashes for the bus. No 60-minute timers on the floor. We close at 22:30 sharp. Our coffees rest two weeks before they hit the bar — and so do you, twice a year.

    How we hire.

    § 01

    Introduce yourself

    Send a short note. Tell us what you make, what you read, and what you'd change about Doppler if you had a week.

    § 02

    A conversation

    A 45-minute call with whoever leads the team you're joining. No whiteboard puzzles.

    § 03

    A day with us

    Come spend half a shift on the floor — paid. You'll see how the room runs and we'll see how you move.

    § 04

    An offer

    Within a week. Written, fair, and never negotiated against another candidate.
